Help Contribute to Stop Extinction

We are here to help animals from going extinct. We need money to do this though. If we can get enough money, we can save animals.

Who Are We?

We are the Foundation of Saving Animals. We save animals that are endangered, threatened, extinct in the wild, and more. We have been able to save multiple animals with funds that are given to us by contributors. We have so far been able to save about 500 Red Pandas, 200 Black Rhinos, 13 Blue-throated Macaws, 50 Sea Otters, and 350 Snow Leopards.
